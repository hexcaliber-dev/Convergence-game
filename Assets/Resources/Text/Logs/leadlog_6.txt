<b>Lead Log #6</b>

Supposedly her screams was picked up in security camera outside the hallway.
 
I couldn’t hear them. I was wearing headphones.
 
No one could hear them. They were all wearing headphones.
 
Those who passed were wearing wireless air buds.
 
We’re all deaf.
 
Her doors were closed, but none of us could see it slightly ajar, with the window that framed the grand oil painting of death to all that were blind.
